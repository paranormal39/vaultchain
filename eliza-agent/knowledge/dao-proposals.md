# VaultChain DAO Proposal Guidelines

## Proposal Types

### Treasury Management Proposals
- **Purpose**: Fund allocation, strategy changes, emergency actions
- **Timeline**: 1-6 months execution period
- **Requirements**: Risk assessment, allocation breakdown, success metrics

### Development Proposals  
- **Purpose**: Platform improvements, new features, technical upgrades
- **Timeline**: 2-12 months development cycle
- **Requirements**: Technical specification, milestone plan, budget breakdown

### Marketing Proposals
- **Purpose**: Community growth, partnerships, brand awareness
- **Timeline**: 1-3 months campaign period  
- **Requirements**: Target metrics, channel strategy, ROI projections

## Proposal Format
1. **Title**: Clear, descriptive proposal name
2. **Summary**: 2-3 sentence overview
3. **Rationale**: Why this proposal is needed
4. **Implementation**: Step-by-step execution plan
5. **Budget**: Detailed cost breakdown
6. **Timeline**: Key milestones and deadlines
7. **Success Metrics**: How success will be measured
8. **Risk Assessment**: Potential risks and mitigation strategies

## Voting Process
- **Proposal Period**: 7 days for discussion
- **Voting Period**: 5 days for casting votes
- **Quorum**: Minimum 25% of token holders must participate
- **Approval**: Simple majority (>50%) for standard proposals
- **Supermajority**: 67% required for emergency fund access
