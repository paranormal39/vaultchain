# VaultChain DAO Compliance Documentation

## Regulatory Framework

### Privacy Compliance
- All transactions use zero-knowledge proofs via Midnight Network
- Member identities protected through ZK verification
- Voting records anonymous but verifiable
- Treasury operations maintain privacy while ensuring transparency

### Financial Compliance
- Treasury operations must comply with applicable DeFi regulations
- All fund movements logged on-chain for audit purposes
- Regular financial reporting to community required
- External audit recommended annually for treasuries > 100,000 DUST

## Governance Compliance

### Decision Making Process
- All major decisions require community vote
- Emergency actions limited to 5% of treasury without vote
- Treasury manager actions must follow established policies
- Override mechanisms available for emergency situations

### Documentation Requirements
- All proposals must be documented and archived
- Voting records maintained permanently on-chain
- Treasury reports published monthly
- Policy changes require 2/3 majority approval

## Risk Management Compliance
- Risk assessments mandatory for all proposals > 1,000 DUST
- Diversification requirements prevent single-point-of-failure
- Emergency procedures documented and tested quarterly
- Insurance considerations for large treasury holdings

## Audit Trail Requirements
- Complete transaction history maintained
- Decision rationale documented for all treasury actions
- Regular compliance reviews scheduled
- External audit access provided when requested
