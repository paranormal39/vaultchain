# KYC and Compliance Framework

## Privacy-First KYC Solutions

### Zero-Knowledge KYC
- **Selective Disclosure**: Share only necessary information
- **Credential Verification**: Prove attributes without revealing data
- **Biometric Privacy**: Encrypted biometric verification
- **Decentralized Identity**: Self-sovereign identity solutions

### Regulatory Requirements by Jurisdiction

#### United States
- **BSA Compliance**: Bank Secrecy Act requirements
- **FinCEN Guidance**: Virtual currency regulations
- **State Money Transmitter**: Licensing requirements
- **OFAC Sanctions**: Prohibited persons screening

#### European Union
- **GDPR Compliance**: Data protection requirements
- **5AMLD/6AMLD**: Anti-money laundering directives
- **MiCA Regulation**: Markets in Crypto-Assets
- **Travel Rule**: Transaction information sharing

#### Asia-Pacific
- **Singapore MAS**: Payment Services Act
- **Japan JVCEA**: Self-regulatory organization rules
- **Hong Kong SFC**: Securities and futures regulations
- **Australia AUSTRAC**: Anti-money laundering requirements

## Risk-Based Approach

### Customer Risk Categories
- **Low Risk**: Verified individuals, small transactions
- **Medium Risk**: Business accounts, moderate volumes
- **High Risk**: PEPs, high-volume traders, sanctioned countries

### Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
- Source of funds verification
- Beneficial ownership identification
- Ongoing transaction monitoring
- Regular risk reassessment

## Technology Solutions

### Automated KYC Platforms
- **Jumio**: AI-powered identity verification
- **Onfido**: Document and biometric verification
- **Chainalysis**: Blockchain analytics and compliance
- **Elliptic**: Crypto transaction monitoring

### Privacy-Preserving Technologies
- **zk-SNARKs**: Zero-knowledge proofs for compliance
- **Selective Disclosure**: Minimal data sharing
- **Homomorphic Encryption**: Compute on encrypted data
- **Secure Multi-Party Computation**: Collaborative verification

## Compliance Monitoring

### Transaction Monitoring
- Real-time risk scoring
- Pattern recognition algorithms
- Threshold-based alerts
- Manual review processes

### Reporting Requirements
- **SAR Filing**: Suspicious Activity Reports
- **CTR Filing**: Currency Transaction Reports
- **Travel Rule**: Cross-border information sharing
- **Regulatory Notifications**: Breach and incident reporting

## Data Protection

### Privacy by Design
- Data minimization principles
- Purpose limitation
- Storage limitation
- Security by default

### Cross-Border Data Transfers
- Adequacy decisions
- Standard contractual clauses
- Binding corporate rules
- Certification mechanisms

## Audit and Documentation

### Record Keeping
- Customer identification records (5+ years)
- Transaction records and monitoring
- Training and policy documentation
- Audit trails and system logs

### Regular Audits
- Internal compliance reviews
- External audit requirements
- Regulatory examinations
- Penetration testing
