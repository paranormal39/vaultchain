# DAO and LLC Formation Guide

## DAO Formation Best Practices

### Legal Structure Options
- **Unincorporated DAO**: Simple but limited legal protection
- **DAO LLC**: Combines DAO governance with LLC legal framework
- **Foundation + DAO**: Non-profit foundation managing DAO operations
- **Cooperative DAO**: Member-owned cooperative structure

### Key Formation Steps
1. **Define Purpose**: Clear mission and objectives
2. **Choose Jurisdiction**: Wyoming, Delaware, or international options
3. **Draft Governance**: Token distribution, voting mechanisms
4. **Legal Documentation**: Operating agreements, bylaws
5. **Regulatory Compliance**: Securities law, tax obligations

## LLC Formation for DAOs

### Wyoming DAO LLC Benefits
- Legal recognition of DAO structure
- Limited liability protection for members
- Flexible governance structures
- Clear regulatory framework

### Formation Requirements
- **Articles of Organization**: File with Wyoming Secretary of State
- **Operating Agreement**: Define member rights and governance
- **Registered Agent**: Wyoming address requirement
- **Initial Report**: Due within 60 days of formation

### Ongoing Compliance
- Annual reports to Secretary of State
- Tax filings (typically pass-through taxation)
- Maintain registered agent and address
- Keep governance records updated

## KYC/AML Compliance

### Know Your Customer (KYC)
- Identity verification for members
- Address verification requirements
- Enhanced due diligence for high-risk members
- Ongoing monitoring obligations

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
- Transaction monitoring systems
- Suspicious activity reporting
- Record keeping requirements (5+ years)
- Compliance officer designation

### Privacy-Compliant KYC
- Zero-knowledge identity verification
- Selective disclosure protocols
- Decentralized identity solutions
- Regulatory-approved privacy methods

## Securities Law Compliance

### Token Classification
- **Utility Tokens**: Access to services/products
- **Security Tokens**: Investment contracts requiring registration
- **Governance Tokens**: Voting rights, potential securities

### Howey Test Considerations
1. Investment of money
2. Common enterprise
3. Expectation of profits
4. From efforts of others

### Compliance Strategies
- Utility-focused token design
- Decentralized launch mechanisms
- Geographic restrictions
- Accredited investor limitations

## International Considerations

### Jurisdiction Shopping
- **Switzerland**: Crypto-friendly regulations
- **Singapore**: Clear regulatory framework
- **Cayman Islands**: Flexible corporate structures
- **Marshall Islands**: DAO-specific legislation

### Cross-Border Compliance
- Multiple regulatory regimes
- Tax treaty considerations
- Member jurisdiction restrictions
- Regulatory arbitrage risks
