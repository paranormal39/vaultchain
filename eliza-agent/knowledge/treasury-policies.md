# VaultChain DAO Treasury Policies

## Risk Management Framework

### Risk Levels
- **Low Risk**: Emergency reserves > 40%, Conservative allocation
- **Medium Risk**: Emergency reserves 25-40%, Balanced allocation  
- **High Risk**: Emergency reserves < 25%, Requires immediate action

### Allocation Guidelines
- **Emergency Fund**: Minimum 25%, Maximum 50%
- **Development**: 20-45% based on roadmap priorities
- **Marketing**: 5-25% based on growth phase
- **Strategic Reserves**: 10-30% for opportunities

## Approval Thresholds
- Proposals > 5,000 DUST: Requires DAO vote
- Proposals 1,000-5,000 DUST: Treasury manager discretion
- Proposals < 1,000 DUST: Automatic approval if within policy

## Compliance Requirements
- All proposals must include risk assessment
- Monthly treasury health reports required
- Quarterly strategy reviews mandatory
- Emergency fund cannot be allocated without supermajority vote
